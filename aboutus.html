About Us

Company Overview

Brickhouse Tools was founded in early 2002, starting out as a basic drill distribution store. Later on we realized that we could expand our business by going online. In 2004 Brickhouse Tools went all out, and allocated all of its resources into building a website and switches to completely online. Eventually Brickhouse Tools expanded to include others power tools as well as the best drills on the planet. Today Brickhouse has expanded further to produce its own brand name drills and power tools. Switching from a distribution store to a supplier.

Who we are

Here at Brickhouse Tools we strive to put the customer first. We keep our staff relatively small so that we can emphasize community and the importance of customer service.

Our Guarantee

Brickhouse Tools guarantees the best of quality drills for cheap prices and the most convenient drill shopping on the Web!
